Expanding Your Horizons

Project Details

Over 4 years, single handedly created and managed all operational procedures of a small gourmet hotdog cart/ catering business. Managed scheduling of extensive catering and event calendar. Excelled in customer satisfaction and relations. Menu creation and product testing. Using customer feedback to maintain quaility control via Yelp. Brand development, logo design and created promotional materials like koozies, stickers and T-shirts. Produced original promotional content for Facebook, Instagram and Youtube, developing a regular local following, even appearing on national television. With the community’s support, Frankie Dogs Gourmet was awarded “Best Of” courtesy of Broward county’s local New Times. Best Hotdog - 2014 Best Late night Dining - 2015 Developing FDG gave me first hand experience in turning a small idea into a recognizable venture, the entrepreneurial side of business was the true passion. With a new local owner, Frankie Dogs Gourmet remains a Fort Lauderdale favorite giving a little sparkle of independent culture where ever it rolls up..
