Frankie Dogs Gourmet

Project Details

Over 5 years, I single handedly created and managed all operational procedures of a small gourmet hotdog cart/ catering business. At its peak, I managed and scheduled an extensive 6 day a week catering and event calendar from corporate luncheons to late night curb side pop-ups. With my main focus on quality and customer service, I developed a solid online reputation.
See Frankie Dogs Gourmet Latest Site

With a focus on brand development, I designed menus, logos and promotional materials like koozies, stickers and T-shirts. As well as produced original promotional content for Facebook, Instagram and Youtube, developing a regular local following, even appearing on national television.

With the community’s support, Frankie Dogs Gourmet was awarded “Best Of” courtesy of Broward county’s local New Times.

Best Hotdog - 2014

Best Late Night Dining - 2015

Developing FDG gave me first hand experience in turning a small idea into a recognizable venture, the entrepreneurial side of business was the true passion. With a new local owner, Frankie Dogs Gourmet remains a Fort Lauderdale favorite giving a little sparkle of independent culture where ever it rolls up.
